Powerlifting 101

Powerlifting is made up of 3 core lifts: squat, bench, and deadlift.

Lifting heavy things and putting them back down can be very good for your health, when performed with proper form. Explore this site to learn more about the magic of SBD.

How to Bench

  1. Lie down on bench with eyes underneath the bar.
  2. Squeeze your shoulder blades in and down your back. Raise your chest while arching your lower back, and tighten your upper back.
  3. Grip bar around ring marks and externally rotate the shoulders. Take a big breath in and brace, unrack the bar.
  4. Lower down slowly, keeping the wrists straight, driving the elbows out at 45-degrees and touch the bar to your lower chest.
  5. Press up, while engaging back, lats and chest. Re-rack.
