Powerlifting 101

Powerlifting is made up of 3 core lifts: squat, bench, and deadlift.

Lifting heavy things and putting them back down can be very good for your health, when performed with proper form. Explore this site to learn more about the magic of SBD.

How to Squat

  1. Stand straight with your feet hip-width apart, directly under your hips, and place the bar on your back - engaging the back and lats.
  2. Take a deep breath in, tightening your stomach and back muscles, bracing your core. Lift the bar off the rack and step back into position.
  3. Lower down with control, as if sitting, while driving knees outwards.
  4. Once your thighs are parallel to the floor, you have hit depth. Straighten your legs to come up while squeezing from the glutes.
